### 1. Bridge Guardian — Structural Health Monitoring System
*An autonomous computer vision system designed for real-time monitoring and structural analysis of bridges to identify cracks, corrosion, and assess structural integrity.*
* **Core Features:** Real-time crack detection using OpenCV visual filters, automated sensor-data fusion logic, and instant alerts powered by FastAPI.
* **Impact:** Automates high-risk inspections, improving speed by 60% and eliminating manual climbing hazards for structural engineers.
* **Tech Stack:** `Python` • `OpenCV` • `FastAPI` • `MediaPipe` • `NumPy`
* [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Shrishti08raghav)

### 2. AI Idea & Startup Generator
*An LLM-powered agentic platform that analyzes market trends, generates viable startup concepts, constructs business model canvases, and drafts product-market fit reports.*
* **Core Features:** Dynamic TAM/SAM market size estimation, automatic Business Model Canvas drafting, and competitor analysis with unique selling proposition (USP) mapping.
* **Impact:** Accelerates the initial business ideation phase from days to seconds using data-backed generative insights.
* **Tech Stack:** `LangChain` • `OpenAI API` • `FastAPI` • `Python` • `Vercel`
* [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Shrishti08raghav)

### 3. Phantom Mouse — Gesture-Controlled Virtual Interface
*A touchless human-computer interface leveraging real-time computer vision to map hand gestures directly to OS-level cursor movements, clicks, and scroll actions.*
* **Core Features:** Multi-landmark hand tracking, custom coordinate mapping, and mathematical smoothing filters to eliminate hand jitter and improve tracking precision.
* **Impact:** Provides a hygienic touchless interface for public displays and serves as an accessibility tool for individuals with mobility challenges.
* **Tech Stack:** `Python` • `OpenCV` • `MediaPipe` • `PyAutoGUI` • `NumPy`
* [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Shrishti08raghav)

### 4. AI Crop & Disease Detection System
*An intelligent agricultural computer vision application designed to identify crop varieties, analyze health status, and detect plant anomalies.*
* **Core Features:** Automated disease classification with severity metrics, visual heatmaps showing nutrient or water stress, and automated treatment recommendations.
* **Impact:** Empowers agricultural managers to make proactive decisions, boosting crop protection and potential yields by up to 20%.
* **Tech Stack:** `Python` • `OpenCV` • `TensorFlow` • `Pandas` • `NumPy`
* [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Shrishti08raghav)

### 5. Book Hub — Semantic Discovery Platform
